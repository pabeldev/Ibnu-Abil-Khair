# Ibnu Abil Khair | Ecos De Papel 🎙️

A silent world where words find their voice.

Welcome to the official repository of **Ecos De Papel**, a minimalist and soul-stirring personal portfolio dedicated to the poetic essence and deep reflections of **Ibnu Abil Khair**. This project is designed to provide a serene digital space for listeners and readers to connect with his journey.

---

✨ Key Features

- **Atmospheric Dark UI:** A deep, obsidian-themed interface designed for focus and emotional resonance.
- **Poetic Storytelling:** Custom sections crafted to showcase thoughts, prayers, and whispers of the soul.
- **Fully Responsive:** Optimized for a seamless experience across mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
- **Interactive Elements:** Smooth scroll animations, floating navigation, and integrated social call-to-actions.
- **Optimized for Sharing:** Custom Open Graph meta tags for professional previews on WhatsApp, Facebook, and Twitter.
